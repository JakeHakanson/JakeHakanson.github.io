About Me

Jake Hakanson is a Sophmore, at the University of Miami where he is studying journalism and sports administration.

Hakanson covers local sports for UM’s WVUM student-run radio station as well as the newspaper The Miami Hurricane. The past four years he worked as sports director at WKWZ Syosset’s local radio station. While working there he covered all sport-related events for the station and for the newspaper The Pulse.

He is an avid sports fan supporting the Jets, Mets, Knicks, and Islanders. He is a member of Miami’s golf club.

Hakanson graduated from Syosset High School in 2013 with honors in English and social studies. He was also inducted into the National Honor Society. He was a core member of the investment club, newspaper, and radio. He plays clarinet and volunteers at Sunrise Day camp.
